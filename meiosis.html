Meiosis

Meiosis is the production of gametes(male and female sex female)for sexual reproduction.the production of sperm is known as spermotogenisis whilst the production of ovums is known as oogenisis.Spermatogenisis starts off with a spermatogenium that divides mitotically to produce two daughter cells.One of the cells are used to replenish the spermatogia reserves whilst the other cell(primary spermatocyte) undergoes meiosis to produces sperm cells. Oogenisis starts off with the oogenium which divides mitotically to produce two primary oocytes and the process stops. At puberty, a primary oocyte undergoes meiosis to produce a secondary oocyte which when fertilized undergoes meiosis II to produce an ovum. If two secondary oocytes are released,fertilized and implanted in the uterus then fraternal twins are born.They are genectically different.On the other hand if the zygote idvide mitotically into two separate bodies early on during its development,this results in identical twins.
